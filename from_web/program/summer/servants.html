Servant Events

Servants on the Coast
(LCMS Servant Event)
July 7-12, 2013

Open to: Senior High

Servants on the Coast is a week long experience that pairs servant opportunities with fun west coast adventure options. Servants participate in service projects at Mt. Cross and also travel off-site to unique servant opportunities that help the members of our local community. Servants will have a chance to relax and have fun by choosing 1 or 2 special west coast activities: including surfing, kayaking, playing at the beach, a beach bonfire, high ropes and more. To round out this wonderful experience, servants will enjoy a daily Bible study, evening devotions, and group time to help them process this week of service, excitement and spiritual growth. 2013 Servants on the Coast Planning Guide


Special Friends (SF) Servants
August 3-9th, 2013

Open to: Senior high, older adults, families

Serve adults with developmental disabilities in the redwoods of the Santa Cruz Mountains, California. Live out the love of Jesus Christ 24/7 (besides church group time). Help developmentally disabled adults worship God through song and praise, hike, swim, make crafts, ride the Roaring Railroad and more. Special Friends offers individuals an opportunity to gain leadership skills while growing spiritually as servants. Come ready to be thoroughly exhausted in service and relationship. Church-Group Time will include Bible study, processing the day & potential activities (including team building, climbing tower, etc.) Special Friends Servant Planning Guide


Adventures on the Coast
(Mt. Cross Servant Events)
Summer 2013

Open to: Junior & Senior High

Adventures on the Coast is a one week, customizable adventure experience that provides a chance for adventure, service, and spiritual growth. If you are looking for adventure then try surfing lessons, kayaking, a day hike, an overnight backpacking trip, or a day on our ropes course. If you are looking for a chance to serve, then we have several projects located at Mt. Cross or we can set you up with unique servant opportunities that benefit the local community. The great thing about this program that the choice is yours! Your group will also have the chance to enjoy daily Bible studies, morning worships and evening campfires, as well as group time to encourage processes for this great week.

Check out the Adventures on the Coast web page for more information!
