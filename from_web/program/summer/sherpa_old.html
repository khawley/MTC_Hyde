Mt. Cross is Seeking Recommendations for
Pastors and other Professional Church Workers
to serve as "Sherpa" Guides for Summer Staff
Summer 2012

Being a "Sherpa" guide for Mt. Cross Summer Staff is a chance for Pastors/Youth Workers to come and be a part of the Mt. Cross community; as well as a time to be renewed, be refreshed and share your knowledge & experience with the camp community. 

What should a Sherpa expect? You should take time to enjoy the beauty of the Redwoods while you plug into camp life by...

  • Providing a 45-minute interactive, thought provoking devotion/Bible Study for summer staff during daily staff meetings (Monday through Thursday). 
    Optional    : stay for Friday staff meeting (late afternoon) and share an additional 15-minute devotion as a part of our weekly closing staff meeting..

  • Joining (on occasion) cabin groups in a variety of activities. Maybe you have a knack for kickball or know how to make a pencil from a tree branch or want to go on a hike with a cabin.

  • Being available to chat with campers and staff if a special need arises.

  • Assisting staff in Bible Encounters.

  • Taking time for relaxation and renewal.

And it is FREE  … for you and your family (includes lodging and all meals during the week). Arrive by 3pm on Sunday and depart on Friday afternoon.

 

Interested or know someone who would be great?  Please contact the Director of Programs, 831.336.5179 x12 or program@mtcross.org
