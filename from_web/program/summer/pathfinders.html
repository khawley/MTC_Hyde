This program is designed for new and returning campers who are ready for a week of fun and excitement. Pathfinders will enjoy traditional camp activities such as hiking, swimming, archery or creek walking and they will have the chance to try new options like geocache, Balanz Bikes, night hikes and campouts.

Fee includes lodging, meals, staff, summer programs, 5x7 camp photo and more.

Stay two weeks using Connections options (or use Connections for a Sunday pickup time.)
