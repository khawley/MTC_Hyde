Special Friends Camp

Adults with Developmental Disabilities

Special Friends: August 4-9, 2013

Registration Fee: FULL

As a Special Friend, adults with developmental disabilities can experience camp. Special Friend campers live in community with fellow campers, Mt. Cross staff and volunteers. This exciting week lets camper groups decide what activities to participate in with their cabin mates. Activities may include swimming, craft projects, campfires, singing, games, an afternoon ride up the mountain on the Roaring Camp Railroad and more.

Limit: 30 campers per session.
