Kinder Kamp

Children Ages 4-5 overnight with a parent, grandparent, aunt, uncle, etc.

Kinder Kamp: July 20-21, 2012

Registration Fee: $150 for pair

Grab a mom, dad, grandma, grandpa, aunt, uncle, older brother or sister, etc. Kinder Kamp is a great opportunity to experience camp for the first time. Mt. Cross staff will lead campers on a hike, create a time for swimming, sing with adults and kids around the campfire and lead everyone in an exploration of God's Word. Each family pair receives a FREE 5x7 camp photo.
