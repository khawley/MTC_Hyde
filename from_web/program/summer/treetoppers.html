Tree Toppers

Youth Grades 4th-6th & 7th-9th (with high ropes)

  • Tree Toppers 4** (grades 7-9)(with high ropes): July 7-12, 2013
  • Tree Toppers 6** (grades 4-6): July 21-26, 2013
  • Tree Toppers 7** (grades 4-6): July 28-August 2, 2013

*Tree Toppers 1: $305
**Tree Toppers 3-7: $370

Imagine looking up at stars as you sleep under towering Redwood trees in the Mt. Cross Treehouse. Tree Topper campers plan daily schedule that might include: trekking down the creek, working together as a team on the challenge course, building a fort, swimming, crafts, archery or other new ideas your group comes up with.
Each day experience digging into God's Story through Bible Encounter and devotion times. PLUS have the opportunity to cook out several meals. Limited to 24 campers each session.

Fee includes lodging, meals, staff, summer programs, 5x7 camp photo and more.

Stay two weeks using Connections options (or use Connections for a Satuday or Sunday pickup time).
