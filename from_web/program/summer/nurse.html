Volunteer Camp Nurse

Volunteer for 1 week or more

Are you an RN, LPN, or other medical professional?  Do you have a heart for service?  Do you love camp?  Mt. Cross is looking for volunteers who would like to spend a week (or even the whole summer) serving campers (age 8-17) and the Mt. Cross staff medical needs.

Responsibilities include:

  • partnering with the Mt. Cross Health Care Manager,

  • checking in/out camper medication on the first and last day of camp,

  • dispensing medication throughout the week,

  • monitoring campers health and well-being; as well as

  • plugging into the Mt. Cross community (as desired).

Arrive by noon on Sunday (you may arrive Saturday evening) and depart in theafternoon on Friday.

Interested?  Please fill out the Volunteer Camp Nurse Application and return to the Program Director, at program@mtcross.org  Have questions?  Call us at 831.336.5179
