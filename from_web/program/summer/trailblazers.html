*Trailblazers 1: $305

Ready to step up your adventure at Mt. Cross? The Trailblazers program allows campers to grow in community with fellow campers, get up in the trees on Mt. Cross' high ropes course, have a beach adventure day and spend time in God's word and creation. This camp experience allows campers to experience all that camp has to offer.

Fees include lodging, meals, staff, summer programs, and a 5x7 camp photo.
