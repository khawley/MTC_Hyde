Young Explorers

Children Ages 6-8 Weekend Camps

Young Explorers 1: June 15-17, 2012
Young Explorers 7: July 13-15, 2012

Registration Fee: $100

Ready for your first taste of camp? Young Explorers weekend camp is designed to help young campers ease into the camp experience. Imagine singing camp songs around a campfire, having a God Story adventure, hunting for banana slugs, a dip in the pool or making a craft using the natural items God provides.

Fee includes lodging, meals, staff, summer programs, 5x7 camp photo and more.

*Parents with children attending Young Explorers may want to spend the weekend using the Mt. Cross "Sherwood Campground." Although families cannot join the camper groups, they can observe them from a distance. Make campground reservations by contacting the camp office.
