Confirmation Group Registration ONLY with Leader

SHALOM: July 14-19, 2013

Youth Registration Fee: $370
Leader Registration Fee: $200

This week-long confirmation program guides confirmation youth through the themes of SHALOM. Pastors, youth directors, sponsors and counselors work together, intermixing youth from churches during morning teaching times. Afternoons are spent with cabin mates plugging into camp activities. Evenings close with large group singing and devotions.

Fee includes lodging, meals, staff, summer programs, 5x7 camp photo for campers.
