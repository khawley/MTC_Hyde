2013 Summer Camp

Summer Theme: FAITH ALIVE

"Let all that you do be done in Love." 1 Corinthians 16:14


Camps include activities such as swimming, hiking, campfire cookouts, Bible encounters and campfire devotions, and the Challenge Course (high ropes and low ropes elements).

Cabin groups create their activity schedule from a list of age appropriate activities, participate in all camp activities, spend time at the Trading Post and feast on quality camp food including a main entree', and a salad bar with fresh vegetable and fruit selections.

Campers can invite friends, select cabinmates or come alone to meet new friends. Combine several camps with Connections or use Connections for a Saturday or Sunday pick-up.
