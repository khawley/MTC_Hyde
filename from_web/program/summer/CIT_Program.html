Schedule

Week One
-CIT Week 1 is spent training with a Mt. Cross staff member walking you through the skills needed to be a counselor and a leader.

Week Two
-CIT Week 2 is spent in a cabin working alongside a Mt. Cross counselor putting all you learned the previous week into action.

* * * * * * *

Advanced CITs (aCIT) spend the entire 2-3 weeks with campers, reviewing some CIT training sessions as agreed upon with CIT Coordinator.

Junior Counselors (JC) spend 4 weeks with campers assisting a Summer Staff with their cabin. They must have sucessfully completed CIT & aCIT sessions and receive a recommendation from either a counselor they served with or the CIT Coordinator to attend.
