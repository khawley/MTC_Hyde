Bring your tools to fix and repair. Mt. Cross aims to organize specific projects for those with specific skills. Also, bring tools for general repairs, cleaning and grounds work. This weekend is free to all working families who are registered in advance.

*Please note that the Work Weekend is scheduled for arrival on Friday evening, projects all day Saturday, BBQ and Memorial Day programming Sunday, and finishing up projects as needed on Monday.

This is it, the big weekend of the year! You can fight traffic and the rest of California going camping and to the lake, or you can come for a weekend of projects at Mt. Cross to repair and prepare camp for the summer season. Saturday and Monday are spent working, with recreation activities, campfire devotions and rest time in the evening. You must register yourself and/or your family for this event.

There are new and exciting things this year for our Family Work Weekend and Memorial Day programming. This year we have the pleasure of welcoming our local Thrivent Financial chapter to our Memorial Day programs. We will be teaming up with them to help support their local build right here in Santa Cruz! This means a few things will change from previous years.
