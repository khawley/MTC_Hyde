A weekend retreat led by and for youth with adult coaches. Hear high school youth share their faith stories and dive deeply into a plan to connect Jesus with the world we live in.

Youth groups and confirmation groups from across northern California and western Nevada come for an incredible winter event on the Calfornia coast.

Housing & Leaders: All participants will be housed in Evergreen/Redwood dorms and Madrone cabins. These buildings have bunk beds and shared bathroom facilities. Adult leaders will stay with youth from their church and youth from other churches. Each church group needs a male and female adult leader if they bring male & female youth. Must have at least 1 adult leader per 7 youth.
