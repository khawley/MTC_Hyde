**BEFORE YOU REGISTER: PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION**

Welcome Parent’s to Mt. Cross’ NEW registration!
We are very excited to introduce an easy and thorough registration system all online. This means, less paper-work, hassle for group leaders, and simple data import to Mt. Cross. With that, we are now asking for each parent to go to our website and register their camper online. Your church’s group leader will know the exact program and dates to register for. You will need to input the church your camper is going with and the leader’s name in order to be placed in the correct group.

Just follow the instructions below and sign your youth up today!!

1. Click - “Register for JAM” below

2. Create an account (this account will be used for ALL future registrations for your family!!)

3. Register your youth for one of the JAM Sessions (Your group leader will inform you as to which session to sign-up for)

4. Fill-out all the health information online

5. Submit the registration and talk with you group leader about how the church is paying for the retreat (some churches may pay in full and others may collect from the families)

6. Receive a confirmation email that includes a packing list for your camper.

7. Your camper heads to camp in January with no paper documents in hand or to give to the group leader! All of it is completed online already!!
