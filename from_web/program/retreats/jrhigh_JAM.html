JAM Info.

What is JAM? Jr. High youth groups and confirmation groups from across northern California and western Nevada pinch their pennies to attend this incredible winter event on the California coast.
A Confirmation/Jr High Retreat focused on group building, Bible Study, Challenge Course, games, music, inspirational talks, and lots of fun!

TWO JAM DATES: Due to popular demand we will be hosting two sessions for our JAM Retreat this year. Each session will be limited to 75 participants in order to maintain the quality experience that you have come to expect from Mt. Cross. Registration for each session will be on a first come first serve basis, so we encourage group leaders to register early to secure your preferred date.


Housing and Adult Leaders: All particpants will be housed in Evergreen/Redwood dorms and Madrone cabins. These buildings have bunk beds and shared bathroom facilities. Adult leaders will stay with youth from their church and youth from other churches. Each church group needs a male and female adult leader if they bring male & female youth. Must have at least 1 adult leader per 7 youth.
