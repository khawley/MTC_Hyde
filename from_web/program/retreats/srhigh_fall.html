Currently Not Planned

Registration Fees: $104/youth and $44/adult leader if paid or postmarked by ----; $114/youth after ----.

Get FULLY DRENCHED this fall. Each youth group will sail along on a journey, using their own unique map, experiencing their unique hardships and solutions. Leaders should prepare participants for a journey that requires stamina & endurance - physically & emotionally. Come in clothes that can get dirty. Plan for your journey to include hikes high up Mt. Cross' hillsides, sitting at dusty destinations, physically teaming to make sure each member of the group achieves difficult challenges and sharing insights with other teams.

The Journey Begins with your team's registration. If you register by September 15, your team will receive a pre-event training packet.

Who: Highly adventure driven Youth Groups - especially applicable to those incorporating new students into their group.
