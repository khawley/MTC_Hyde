Family Facts & Faith

Kids are more likely to feel they have received the Family Blessing when the family camps together. (The Blessing, Smalley & Trent)

"...kids whose fathers showed high levels of physical play were most popular among their peers...in ways that were non-directive, non-coercive... ...children seem to develop the best social skills when their dads keep the tone of their interactions positive and allow kids to take part in directing the course of play." (John Gottman, Raising an Emotionally Intelligent Child, p. 171)
