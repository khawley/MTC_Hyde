This annual event is much like an open house. Feel free to ask staff for a tour of the camp facilities, experience the food, and enjoy recreation for a few hours. Lunch is served from 2:00-3:30pm. Event ends at 4:00pm.

There are new and exciting things this year for our Memorial Day Weekend programming. This year we have the pleasure of welcoming our local Thrivent Financial Chapter to our Memorial Day programs. We will be teaming up with them to help support their local build right here in Santa Cruz! They will be sponsoring a carnival during the BBQ to raise money for their build!

*Please note that the Work Weekend is scheduled for arrival on Friday evening, projects all day Saturday, BBQ and Memorial programming Sunday, and finishing up projects as needed on Monday.

Download the Acknowledgement of Risk to participate in the afternoon activities

You may also be interested in our Memorial Family Work Weekend, where we work as a group to clean up camp for summer!
