Mt. Cross arranges and leads Jr. High events during the Winter and Spring each year. These annual events support the confirmation age and junior high ministries of Lutheran congregations and their guests.

All programs are for GROUP registration. Adult leaders bring their youth in order to have an investment of time with students, and to engage in their faith life.

Each of these retreats is intended to be experiential. Students and adult leaders will engage in activities that help them explore faith issues and create faith metaphors for their groups.


Build Your Own Fall Starter Retreat: Start your year off with a fall youth retreat that includes group building by using MTC's Team Initiative Program facilitators with your own retreat.
