Cross Ministries' main site is **[located][directions]** in the coastal redwood forest of the **Santa Cruz Mountains**, 20 minutes from the Santa Cruz **beaches**. Mt. Cross offers **[summer camp][summerHome]** and **[retreat][yrrndHome]** opportunities for all ages. Most Mt. Cross directed camps are on the main site and many, like **[Day Camps][daycampHome]**, take place in communities all over northern California. Churches, schools, clubs and other organizations use Mt. Cross as their **[group retreat][rentalsTips]** center by utilizing the Outdoor Conference Center (OCC). Mt. Cross provides **[facilities][facilitiesHome]**, meal service and program support (as desired) to make your group's experience as successful as possible. Many groups take advantage of the **[Challenge Course][TIP]** and/or **[climbing tower][tower]**. Established in 1948, Mt. Cross has a unique **[history][historyHome]** in Northern California.
