Day Camp Ministries

We bring camp to you!


Congregational Based Day Camps

Day Camp is a unique partnership between Mt. Cross and your church or school. Our summer staff come to your congregation for a week of faith-based fun, which includes group games, arts & crafts, worship, community building activities and Bible Encounters based on our summer theme. For children who have completed Kindergarten through 6th grade, Day Camp is a great way to provide summer programming for the kids at your church while reaching out to your community.

Senior Day Camps

Camp isn't just for kids! Senior Day Camp is an exciting, growing program that brings camp to senior citizens, allowing for relationships to develop between our young adult staff and their mature "campers".
