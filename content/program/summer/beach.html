--- title: Beach Camp keywords: off-site, beach, surfing, swimming, kayak, camping description: Beach Camps travel off-site to the beautiful Monterey Bay for surf lessons, boogie boarding, and camping out on the beach. name: Beach Camp --- {% block MainText %} {{ macros.render_camp_info() }}

Mt. Cross is taking camp to the beach! Each day our beach campers will travel with their cabin groups to one of the sunny beaches of Santa Cruz. Highlights of this camp will include boogie boarding, professional surfing lessons, delicious picnic lunches, a special campfire worship on the beach, high ropes and other special activities. This program is perfect for campers who are looking for a new and exciting camp experience.

Limit 24 campers per session.

*Fees include professional surfing instruction and equipment package.
