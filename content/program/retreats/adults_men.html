A retreat for men who may wonder how to engage the stirrings and wonderings of being a person made in God's image and how that awareness affects the way they live out life in family, work and faith commitments. Together we will remember stories, ask questions, listen deeply and engage in different practices that will assist us on the journey. The hope is that at this retreat you may discover some new ways to live each moment fully alive. You may be surprised that you already engage in spiritual practices.

Speaker: Bill Kees

Bill is retired and living in Chico, California with his wife Carolyn. They are members at Faith Lutheran Church. They have 2 children and 7 grandchildren. Their journey in the past 15 years has been filled with many opportunities to walk with men, women and youth as they live in this life as children of God. Both Bill and Carolyn are Spiritual Directors and have a practice in Chico and are affiliated with Bread of Life in Sacramento. It is a blessing to journey with people who are graced by love as they are opened to God in everything that happens.

Musicians: Tim Crabe

Tyler Crabe is a fourth-year M.Div student at Pacific Lutheran Theological Seminary (PLTS). He's from Castro Valley, CA, where he grew up singing in several choirs and musicals at both church and school. He graduated from Pacific Lutheran University in Tacoma, WA in 2009 with a bachelor of musical arts. When he isn't preaching or studying theology, you can usually find Tyler trying a new recipe in the kitchen, lost in a book, or wandering God's creation.
