Come away for a weekend with your fellow sisters in Christ. Mt. Cross is hosting its annual Fall Women's Retreat. We will also spend time singing, laughing and enjoying being women of God. Come for the weekend and be renewed as a woman of God.

Theme:

Over the weekend we will explore together biblical, historical, and cultural understandings of beauty. We will seek to look beyond our culture's narrow definition of beauty as a physical attribute, explore the concept of what it means to be created in God's image, and ask ourselves what it really means to call our savior beautiful.

Speaker: Pastor Sarah Sumner-Eisenbraun

Sarah Sumner-Eisenbraun currently serves as associate and campus pastor at Salem Lutheran Church and School in Glendale, CA. Originally from Rapid City, SD, Sarah became a Lutheran in the spring of 2003 while attending St. Olaf College in Northfield, MN and began seminary just a few months later. She specializes in Children, Youth, and Family Ministry, loves sewing, knitting, reading, and spending time with her husband Kevin and daughter Zoe.


Musician: Erin Armstrong

Erin Armstrong is a second year M.Div student at Pacific Lutheran Theological Seminary (PLTS). She's from Riverside, CA, where she grew up loving singing and church. She graduated from St. Olaf College in Northfield, MN in 2009. She is a veteran of outdoor ministry, having worked at El Camino Pines for six summers and two years. She currently serves as the Youth Director at First Lutheran Church in Palo Alto. She's excited to learn and sing and worship together!
