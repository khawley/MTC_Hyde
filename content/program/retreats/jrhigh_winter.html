Who: Confirmation Classes and Junior High Youth Groups


What: The Junior High Confirmation Weekend is a weekend program for Jr. Hight youth groups and confirmation classes that is designed to let them explore their faith. Facilitated by our dynamic speakers, special music, and thought provoking breakout sessions.


Housing and Adult Leaders: All participants will be housed in our Evergreen and Redwood Dorms or the Madrone Cabins. These buildings have electricity, heating, bunk beds and shared bathroom facilities. Adult leaders will stay with youth from their church and, in some cases, multiple churches may be in the same dorm or cabin.

Each church group must register 1 adult leader for every 7 youth participant; a male and female leader is required of groups bringing youth of both sexes.
